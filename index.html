// The cost of an average website

If your website looks average, customers assume your service is average.

Most people decide whether to trust a business in seconds — long before they call. A weak website quietly costs you the customers you never hear from.

01

They judge before they call

Your website is the first impression, and the first verdict on whether you look reliable and established.

02

Friction loses bookings

If customers must message just to ask prices, location or availability, many simply don't bother.

03

Competitors look easier

They may not be better than you. They may just look sharper and easier to book.

Most small-business websites don't fail because they're ugly. They fail because they don't build trust, explain the offer clearly, or make it easy to book.
